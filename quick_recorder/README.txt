QUICK RECORDER - Keyboard Shortcut Audio Recorder
=================================================

FEATURES:
- Press Ctrl+Shift+R to start recording
- Press Ctrl+Shift+R again to stop
- Shows "Recording started" notification
- Blinking red indicator while recording
- Click indicator to open recordings folder
- Auto-names files: 11Feb2026_15.19CT_91min.wav

SETUP:
1. Double-click "Quick Recorder.bat" on your desktop
2. Keep the window minimized in background
3. Press Ctrl+Shift+R anytime to start recording

RECORDINGS SAVED TO:
C:\Users\YourName\Documents\Sound Recordings\

USAGE:
- Start: Ctrl+Shift+R
- Stop: Ctrl+Shift+R (same hotkey)
- Open folder: Click the red indicator

The recorder runs in the background. Keep it running to use the hotkey anytime!
